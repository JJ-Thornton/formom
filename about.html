Welcome to Mitches Stitches

A sanctuary where the art of quilting meets the warmth of cherished memories.

Founded by Michelle Thornton, Mitches Stitches is more than a business; it's a celebration of passion and craftsmanship inspired by Michelle's mother, who instilled in her a love for the timeless art of quilting.

Michelle, a devoted wife to her husband Rob and a mother of four, has seamlessly woven family values into the fabric of Mitches Stitches. With a deep-seated affection for the craft, Michelle transforms beloved clothes into exquisite quilts, each stitch a testament to her dedication and love for the art.

At Mitches Stitches, the creation of every quilt is a personal journey. It's about taking garments laden with memories and transforming them into unique heirlooms that forge tangible connections to your most treasured moments. These quilts transcend mere products; they are expressions of love, comfort, and a beautiful way of preserving the essence of what matters most.
